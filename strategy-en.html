Our strategy

An industrial platform that brings together industrial businessmen and exporters with representatives from Governmental and semi-governmental sectors concerned with supporting and developing the industry Saudi Arabia and the development of exports to support and promote the national product internally And externally by generating ideas and providing the necessary information and data It promotes public-private partnerships and exchanges information and ideas And proposals to overcome the obstacles and difficulties facing exports Saudi Arabia and the external marketing of the Saudi product, and the platform represents a link Link between industrial investors and governmental and semi-governmental agencies Industrialists of sustainability, growth and export, and it is the voice of the Saudi industrialist

Strategic Goals

  • Bridging the gap between the public and private sectors and bringing points of view closer
  • Increasing intellectual production in the world of industry and exchanging experiences between industrialists And people of experience.
  • Standing on the challenges facing industrialists and exporters and proposing solutions and deliver it to the officials to overcome it.
  • Proposing solutions and presenting ideas that contribute to raising the proportions of goods and products Exported outside the Kingdom
  • Converting innovative ideas and suggestions from the platform into tangible results Through workshops and initiatives.
  • Enhancing effective communication with the governmental and semi-governmental sectors and chambers Commercial and its committees concerned with the Saudi industry and supporting “Made in Saudi Arabia”
  • Familiarity with the industry conditions in all regions of the Kingdom and support investors Potential opinions and data to enter into industrial investments in the Kingdom.
  • Enhancing the national role and responsibility in the relevant voluntary team work Industry and encourage volunteering in this field
  • Providing advice and opinion to the leadership and governmental and semi-governmental agencies for advancement the national economy, promoting development plans, creating jobs and increasing content
  • improving the balance of payments and raising the gross domestic product
  • Presenting recommendations that contribute to strengthening the role of young Saudi men and women aspirants in the Saudi industry and encourages them to participate in the plans
  • Industrial Development in the Kingdom
  • Provide recommendations that contribute to the development of regulations, policies and regulations associated with the industrial and logistical sector in the Kingdom to facilitate investment
  • makers in all fields
  • Studying and researching the continuous development in industry globally and the effects of the industrial revolution Fourth, the upcoming industrial revolutions, digital transformation and digital technologies emerging
  • And its impact on the industry, discuss it and come up with ideas that support the national industry
