The Saudi Industrialists and Exporters Platform was established on the 2nd of Jumada Al-Awwal for the year 1437 AH corresponding to the eleventh of February 2016, And that was under the generous patronage of His Royal Highness Prince Fahd bin Ahmed bin Abd Al-Aziz Al-Saud, which is the first non-voluntary platform for thought and opinion It is a for-profit organization of its kind in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and it is officially registered in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Ministry of Commerce as intellectual property No. 106394

The platform specializes in the affairs of the economic industrial sector and examines the current situation The sector, policies and trends affecting it and suggest innovative initiatives and ideas Industrial, logistical and organizational support industrialists and exporters in the Kingdom Saudi Arabia. The platform is also a forum for exchanging and presenting views and proposals between various industrialists, executives and exporters And representatives from all sectors of the national industry, and the platform includes personalities Different decision-makers, officials and influencers from ministries and government departments in Kingdom and the platform provides a valuable opportunity for communication between the two government sectors And private, as it allows for open discussion and exchange of proposals to develop opportunities Work and find solutions to market challenges and serve the directions of the vision of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Saudi Arabia

The idea of ​​creating a platform for industrialists and exporters

The idea of creating a platform for Saudi industrialists and exporters emerged on a network Social communication during one of the workshops of the Export Development Authority in February 2016 Using technology to facilitate the communication of bright minds Those who are keen on the future of the industrial country and weave ideas directly in the shadow of The concerns of some industrialists and those responsible for attending workshops and conferences, namely Voluntary initiative The idea was presented to the workshop attendees Voluntary work and be purely for the sake of God Almighty. It is not commercial, displaying products and whatnot Like that, to advance our country Then came the Sixth Annual Industrialists' Meeting At the King Fahd Civilization Center in Yanbu Industrial City, under the title “Transformation”. National Conference and Enhancing Industrial Competitiveness” in the presence of a large number of leaders Industrialists and officials in various key and influential parties Industrial development sponsored by the Royal Commission in Yanbu on May 25th 2016, with the announcement of the blessed Vision 2030, the initiative was also presented and met The idea was well received by industrial businessmen and investors The participating industrialists, then the forum expanded gradually, praise be to God Later, a blessing for the idea from the Ministry of Energy, Industry and Mineral Resources At that time, the Royal Commission in Jubail and Yanbu and the Export Development Authority Saudi Arabia and the Saudi Organization for Standardization, Metrology, Quality and Assemblies Industrial and industrial committees in the Council of Saudi Chambers and the Development Authority Saudi exports in the regions of the Kingdom and the rest of the economic sectors official.

The purpose of establishing the platform

The zakat of knowledge is working to spread it, and one of the enjoining the truth is to spread what is useful People in their livelihood and the elevation of their homelands and the goal of the platform of industrialists and exporters The Saudis is the exchange of knowledge by raising the level of volunteer work From leaders in the public and private sectors, through posts and dialogues Real in producing a series of sober industrial and economic scientific discussions to turn To targeted national applications in accordance with the directives of the blessed Vision 2030 and support The efforts of the state - may God protect it - and the activation of true citizenship among its citizens in Advancing and developing the wheel of economic development to create a competitive industrial environment And raising the name of the homeland high, Made in Saudi Arabia, in international forums.
